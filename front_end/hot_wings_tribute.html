Do Buffaloes have wings? Yes, as you can see here:
Wait, what are these so called buffalo wings? Chicken wings that have been deep fat fried and coated with a signature spicy hot sauce, served with bleu cheese dressing and celery sticks
Where did they originate and why Buffalo? They have nothing to do with the large, grassland dwelling mammals, but have a lot to do with a restaurant in Buffalo, New York, called the Anchor Bar.
